Parselmouth someone that can speak Parseltongue (the language of snakes)
Squib someone that has a witch and wizard for parents but has no magical powers themselves
Unspeakables people that work at the Ministry of Magic in the Department of Mysteries
Terms for Things in the Wizarding World
Firebolt the fastest broomstick model
Omniocculars a device similar to binoculars but that you can pause, rewind, and zoom in
Pensive a stone basin you can empty your thoughts into
Remembral a small, clear ball that has smoke inside that turns red when you forget something
Terms for Ways to Travel
Apparate the form of transportation for witches and wizards in which they can go anywhere they want to by mastering the 3 D's: destination, deliberation, and determination; it makes a loud cracking noise; wizards must have a license and be 17 or older in order to apparate legally, and at Hogwarts, they start giving lessons to those interested in their 6th year
Floo Network the transportation of people through fireplaces using Floo Powder (the powder which you throw into the fireplace while standing in it to go to wherever you say out loud)
Hogwarts Express the train which Hogwarts students take to get to Hogwarts
Portkey an item which brings whoever touches it at the proper time to where it is made to go – an item can be turned into a portkey by using the incantation Portus – gives the sensation of something jerking suddenly directly behind the naval – glows bright blue when it is about to leave
